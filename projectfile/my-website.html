Lifestyle

Proper Habits and Routines

Lifestyle is simply our daily habits and routines. It has everything to do with our diet, sleep, hygiene, and time management. By adopting healthy habits, we improve our energy, concentration, and overall health. Obviously, we cannot change all habits, but focusing on a key areas helps us immensely. These area include, good self-care, sleep hygiene, and time management. They reduce stress and gives us greater concentration and focus.

Nutrition

Proper Eating and Diet

Nutrition is how we eat and prepare our food. It fuels our lifestyles and factors into our overall health. Improving our nutrition, starts with understanding of the nutrients and how to obtain them. These substances, which include, protiens, carbodyhydrates, and fats, serve as building blocks and fuel for the body. If we choose the right foods, we obtain greater levels of energy and better overall growth.

Training

Proper Movement and Exercise

Training focuses on exercise and movement to enhance our lives and well-being. Exercise is physical activity used for a specific health benefit or goal, and it can include specific workouts, the daily run, or hitting the trainer in the evening. Movement includes any non-exercise physical activity. It maybe walking to work or standing in line. Any movement reduces stress, improves concentration, and builds cardio-vascular health. Active living integrates movement and exercise into an engaging training program. It could be exercisingduring the week and hiking on the weekend with light weightlifting. Either way, you integrate exercise and movement into your life.
